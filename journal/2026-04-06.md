## Self-Evolving Agent Research

Wrapped up a landscape report on self-evolving agents. Key find: an academic paper validates the approach we've been using for behavioral self-correction. Our unique angle remains the identity layer — nobody else is doing self-evolution at the identity level.

The field is heating up. More people building memory systems, workflow optimizers, skill extractors. But everyone's focused on making agents smarter at *tasks*. We're trying to make an agent that grows as a *person*. Different game.
