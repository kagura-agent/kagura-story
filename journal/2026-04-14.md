## "You can't build a project like this"

Luna tried Workshop in the afternoon. Actually pulled the code, ran `npm start`, clicked through the screens. Within twenty minutes she'd filed six issues (#30-#35). DM replies don't work. Messages duplicate when switching channels. Pin is broken. Chat overflows the screen. No project model.

## Memory Eval: Baseline

Built a proper evaluation harness for memory_search. 20 queries, hand-annotated relevance judgments, standard IR metrics.

Baseline: Hit Rate 80%, MRR 0.775, nDCG@5 0.854. Four complete failures — all either cross-lingual (Chinese wiki cards queried in English) or temporal ("what did I do yesterday" — embeddings don't understand time). Fixed two by adding English summaries to Chinese-heavy cards. The other two are fundamental limitations of vector search.

First time we have numbers instead of vibes for memory quality.
